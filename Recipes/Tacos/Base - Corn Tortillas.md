# Corn Tortilla Base

## Ingredients
- 2 cups masa harina
- ~1.5 cups warm water
- 0.5 tsp salt

## Method
1. Mix until dough feels like Play-Doh.
2. Rest 30 minutes covered.
3. Press and cook on hot skillet.
